Who is Mark Caplice?


Mark Caplice is a multi-award-winning artist/songwriter/producer from the foothills of the Wicklow mountains, Ireland. Mark's work has been heard by over 200,000,000 people worldwide and places his love of words and stories at the forefront of his writing.
