Mark’s debut hit single “Catch a Tear” was accompanied by a truly inventive video captured by Kodaline’s Jason Boland in 2021, whilst the popular follow-up single titled “All In Your Head” (2022) was recorded in U2’s Windmill Lane and features Elbow’s drummer Rick Jupp behind an infectious, hook-filled anthem. Both singles received extensive support across Irish and U.K. radio including live interviews and performances on RTÉ Radio 1 and BBC Scotland.
